How to play

  • Click on the start button for a quick game!
  • In order to move you have to use W,A,S D keys just like in most third person games.
  • W = Up , S = Down , A = Left , D = Right
  • The edges of the grill work like portals, you only die when the snake touches itself (yeah, kinda ambiguous.. but it is what it is).
  • Eat as much ghosts as you can! this will increase your score (wait.. wasn't that pacman or something? yeah whatever).
